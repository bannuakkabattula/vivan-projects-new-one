Disaster Relief & Rehabilitation Program

Standing strong with communities during their toughest times

Our Mission

In times of crisis, Vivan Educational Society takes immediate action to deliver critical relief, rebuild lives, and strengthen community resilience. Our dedicated volunteers and partners ensure that affected families receive timely assistance, recovery aid, and long-term support to restore stability.

Our Relief & Rehabilitation Efforts

Flood Relief Operations

Rapid deployment of emergency teams providing food, shelter, and medical care in flood-affected regions.

Earthquake Recovery

Assisting communities in rebuilding homes and essential infrastructure after devastating earthquakes.

Cyclone Response

Providing rapid aid, rehabilitation kits, and sustainable housing for cyclone-hit coastal families.

Fire Disaster Support

Delivering emergency essentials and livelihood assistance to families affected by fire outbreaks.
